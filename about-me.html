About Me

Me taking photos in the pool at the swimming carnival

Why did I make this site?

I made this site because I wanted to show people the places I have found in Sydney, and the experiences that I have had. I am a 15 year old student from Killarney Heights High School

I love taking photos and trying to visually describe a story or subject. Ever since I was 10, I was exploring in the bush in Oxford Falls behind my house, trying to find places of interest and places to photograph. From 7 years old I have had a youtube channel, making videos and edits, constantly uploading and learning more

When I got my first real dslr when I was 12, thats when I really got into photography. At Killarney I have photographed events like cross country and the swimming carnival, also taking video. I think doing these things for school is fun.

I had been thinking of this idea for and while, and thought it would be cool to make an explorer guide to the unkown places in Sydney, the secret areas. Everytime I am exploring or finding a new place, I always have my camera with me, to take photo and video, to document the location, and importantly, how to get there. I plan to add more content to this website about the many more locations that I have found, with descriptive content. I enjoy skating, bike riding and surfing.
